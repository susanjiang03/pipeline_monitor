About News Aggregator

What is this News Aggregator?
This news aggregator gathers articles from RSS links and displays them for you to view.

Why and what for?
This News Aggregator is one of two parts of our Pipeline Monitor project. It is the process that we chose to monitor.

How do I use it?
On the main page, you can see a plethora of articles. The articles come from either a category or a newspaper. If the title and description of the article are of interest to you, you can click on the title and it will lead you to the source of the article.

What else?
Additionally, you can also go to the 'Create you own feed' page and pick only categories or newspapers that you're interested in. You can also provido a RSS URL and it will get the articles for you.
